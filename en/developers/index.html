# Main Concepts

# Newland AIDC

Newland AIDC is a professional manufacturer and supplier of bar code reading equipment, automatic identification products and electronic high-tech products.

# Hardware equipment

At present, the intelligent commercial hardware mainly promoted by Newland AIDC includes nls-mt90, nls-nft10, nls-mt60e, etc. The equipment is used in a series of business scenarios including logistics, warehousing, etc.

# Application market

There is an application market within the company, through which partners can distribute their applications to devices in large scale. Some partners can have their own managed application market. By default, their device users can only install applications for their devices through the application market.

# Partners

In the registration partner audit, automatic identification of the company will set the corresponding role for the registrant. There are currently three types of partners:

# 1. Platform partners

It can develop the application of adaptive device, manage its own unique application market, control the application market to only appear the application you want, or upload the application. The uploaded application can appear in its own application market without audit. By default, the Newland AIDC can review and decide whether the application is downloaded in other application markets, and platform partners can also set their own Apps don't appear in other app markets.

# 2. Ordinary developers

The application can be developed and uploaded to the application library of Newland AIDC, which can be downloaded by ordinary users after being checked and approved by Newland AIDC.

# 3. Common channel partners

You can maintain a unique application market for your devices. You can only select the applications you want in the application market, and you can't upload applications.

Each partner can register an account number in the partner platform, and has its own operation background. In principle, each device of Newland AIDC will be bound with a partner account when it is sold. The Newland AIDC will provide some functions and authority control services for the granularity of partners.
