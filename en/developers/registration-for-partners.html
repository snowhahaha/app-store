# Registration For Partners

# Description of registration process

  1. Open the partner platform website: http://172.27.0.35 :8080/admin (opens new window), click "register now". It is recommended to use the function of partner platform through Google browser.

  1. Input the mobile phone number and verification code in the input box of the registration page, and click "next".

  1. Fill in the user name and password, and click "register now" to pop up the information of "successful registration".

  1. After activation, log in to the partner page and complete the relevant information (all fields must be filled in correctly and completely). Please submit the business license for company identity and click "save". Waiting for automatic identification company audit (audit time is one working day).

  1. Automatic identification company will assign corresponding roles to users during audit, and you will become a formal partner. You can use this account to log in to the partner platform.
